Changelog

LensLog is currently a free TestFlight beta. This is what's new, most recent first. Once it's on the App Store, released versions will appear at the top, with the beta history below.

Beta

1.0 Beta · July 6, 2026

A big step up from the previous build.

Setup

  • A redesigned setup flow built around your actual contact boxes — left and right eye, with how many blister packs you have left.

Inventory

  • Add contact boxes right from the Inventory tab: quick Left, Right, or Shared, add the matching box for the other eye, and type box counts directly.
  • Every open box shows an L / R sticker and the prescription for that eye; finished boxes tuck away at the bottom.

Editing

  • Edit and delete anything — prescriptions, exam visits, and doctors — from its detail page or a swipe.

Reminders

  • The lens-change reminder is now two moments: an evening "take them out tonight" on your lenses' last day, plus an optional next-morning "start a fresh pair." Each reminder type has its own section in Settings.

The eye

  • New options for the on-screen eye: a "strain when overdue" toggle, and daily lenses now stay clear.

Fixes

  • Fixed an iCloud-sync error some testers saw, edits that could be lost when going back, and a handful of display and accessibility issues.

LensLog is a tracking tool, not medical advice. For anything about your eyes or vision, talk to your eye care professional.
